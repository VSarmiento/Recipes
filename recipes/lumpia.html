Lumpia Shanghai Recipe

Lumpia Shanghai is a Filipino dish that consists of small, deep-fried spring rolls filled with a savory mixture of ground pork, vegetables, and sometimes shrimp or other meats. It's a popular appetizer and is known for its crispy exterior and flavorful filling.

    Ingredients Needed

  • 1 lb of ground pork
  • half an onion
  • 4 cloves of garli
  • less than half a carrot
  • half tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp knorr seasoning
  • green onion
  • celery
  • 1 egg
  • oil

    Preperation Steps

  1. Use grinder to easily cut vegetables
  2. Mash eggs in a large bowl
  3. Add onion, garlic, carrot, green onion, celery in the egg bowl and mix well
  4. add salt, black pepper, knorr season, liquid season into same bowl and mix
  5. Add pork and mix well

    Cooking Steps

  1. Heat up oil in a large pan
  2. Add mixture into lumpia wrappers
  3. Fry until golden brown
  4. Finished
